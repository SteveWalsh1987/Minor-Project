About CookED

What is CookED?

CookEd has been developed by a team of five individuals who are passionate about children learning how to cook and to understanding the importance of a well balanced diet. The team consists of parents and representatives from education and food industries. Together they have put together a site committed to demonstrating to children how to prepare recipes and learn kitchen safety.
