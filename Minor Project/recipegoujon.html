Chicken Goujons


Ingredients

  • 200g breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon of plain flour
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Preparation

  • Preparation time - Less than 30 minutes
  • Cooking time - 30 – 35 minutes
  • Serves 4

Cooking

  • Ask an adult to turn the oven on to 190C/375F/Gas 5, preheat for 10 minutes
  • Cover a baking tray with baking parchment
  • Season the flour by putting it into a bowl and adding the salt and pepper
  • Cut the chicken breasts into thick chunks – you should get about 6 chunks from each breast
  • Crack the eggs into another bowl and beat


  • Put the breadcrumbs into a third bowl
  • Dip a piece of chicken first into the flour and then into the egg mixture and finally into the breadcrumbs until each piece is fully coated in breadcrumbs
  • Place each Gougon onto the baking tray
  • Cook in the oven for 30 – 35 minutes. Remove when they are golden brown
  • Serve with ketchup, mayonnaise or barbeque sauce
  • For a real school treat serve in a soft white roll in your lunchbox!
