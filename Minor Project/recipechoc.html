Chocolate Sponge Cake


Preparation

  • Preparation time - Less than 30 minutes
  • Cooking time - 30 – 60 minutes
  • Serves 8

Ingredients

  • Spoon of butter for greasing
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 90g self raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • Raspberry or Strawberry Jam and Chocolate Sprinkles to decorate

Cooking

  • Ask an adult to turn the oven on to 170C/325F/Gas 3, preheat for 10 minutes
  • Grease a 20cm cake tin with butter and line with greaseproof paper
  • Crack the eggs into a large mixing bowl and add the sugar Mix the egg and sugar together (with an electric mixer if you have one) until the mixture is pale and thick. This may take 10 minutes
  • Sift the flour and cocoa into the mixture and fold it in very gently and slowly


  • Pour the mixture into the prepared tin
  • Bake it in the oven for 15 – 20 minutes until it is risen and firm
  • Remove from the oven with the help of an adult and turn the tin upside down to take the cake out of the tin and onto a wirerack.
  • Peel off the greaseproof paper and leave the cake to cool When cool spread a thin layer of jam on top and decorate with sprinkles
  • Cut into slices and enjoy!
  • Serve with ketchup, mayonnaise or barbeque sauce
  • Hide the leftovers or the adults will eat it all!
